Project Level Adjustment Factors:

Technology used?
What is the Domain level complexity?
Are teams co-located or geographically distributed?
Is Progression test automation infrastructure available?
Is Regression test automation infrastructure available?
Availability of pre-scripted automated regression test suites (i.e. existing automation test coverage)?
Is Build and Deployment infrastructure automated?
What is the Test Management effort?
What is the Test Data and/or Test Documentation preparation effort?
What is the Test Environment preparation effort?
How is the Network connectivity?
Is Pro-active monitoring / health check system infrastructure available?
How is the support from Client management?
How is the support from Vendor management?
Availability of Product / Business Owner's?
Are Product/Business Owners aware of agile principles and practices?
Availability of Support team's?
How is the Onsite or Nearshore coordinator's role and responsibilities?
Does the Testers have Read (/Write) access to core systems (i.e. Servers, Source Code, Database, etc)?
How is the team's Domain knowledge?
What is the level of re-usability of manual tests in subsequent sprints?
Are Testers aware of agile principles and practices?
How is the collaboration with Customers, Approval processes, Receptivity for feedback (Stand-Up, Retrospective, Customer Feedback)?
What is the Defect Turn around Time (Dependencies with Dev, Environment issues, etc.)?
What is the requirement Re-factoring and User Story set back effort?
Testing cycles (1 or 2 rounds of testing)?
